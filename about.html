Meet Our Managing Director

Mrs. Marian Okosun

With over two decades of experience in the sewing industry, Mrs. Marian Okosun has transformed Maomay Sewing Machines from a modest startup into one of Nigeria's premier sewing equipment providers. Her journey began with a simple vision: to empower Nigerian fashion entrepreneurs with quality tools and unwavering support.

Under her leadership, Maomay Sewing Machines has grown from a small shop in Ago Palace to a trusted name in the industry, serving thousands of customers across Nigeria. Mrs. Yusuf's commitment to quality, customer satisfaction, and technical excellence has set new standards in the industry.

"Our success is built on trust and relationships. Every machine we sell, every customer we serve, adds to our story of empowering Nigerian fashion entrepreneurs," says Mrs. Yusuf. Her hands-on approach and deep understanding of both technical and business aspects have been instrumental in building Maomay's reputation for excellence.
